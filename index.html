Spirit Biobank

An open source
study / biosample management software

November 2018 - Fork
Spirit is a free software, that was developed for Actelion Pharmaceuticals and Idorsia Pharmaceuticals. Because of its success, it was decided to fork the open source version and develop it internally only. For further development or consultancy on the open source version, please contact spirit-bio@outlook.com
